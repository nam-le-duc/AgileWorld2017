XP Day “Prelude”

XP Day is an international conference on eXtreme Programming and Agile process, which provides a great chance for techies to share their passion on Agile practices, exchange new practical skills, hands-on exercise and extreme experience.

XP Day takes place around the world annually, making immense positive impacts on community. Together with the pulse of this, XP Day Vietnam 2015 is going to be an outstanding event in year, packed with high-quality tutorials, activities, workshops, and keynote speeches from experts of Agile Tour Group, Java Communities and other influential software companies around Asia.

XP Day Vietnam 2015 is a 1-day tour

Apr 18th, 2015 at Danang, Duy Tan University
